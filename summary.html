Summary

Overall Computer Networking is making,programming, and computing things like items or devices to send data to each other. Not only just send but receive and exchange data along with information. This can be done by using physical wires, or without them and be wireless. This can provide increased speed with internet and more bandwith. It can make things smoother and overall just better whether if your using wired networks or wireless networks.
