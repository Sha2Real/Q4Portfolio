Wireless Based Systems

Communication without cables — connecting the world through airwaves.

Description

Wireless systems use radio waves, microwaves, or satellite signals to transmit data through the air without physical cables.

Function

They power mobile phones, Wi-Fi, GPS, and satellite communication, enabling mobility and global connectivity.
