Fiber Based Systems

Ultra-fast data transmission using light.

Description

Fiber optics use glass or plastic strands to transmit data as light pulses. They provide extremely high bandwidth and fast internet speeds.

Function

Commonly used for high-speed broadband and long-distance communication, fiber is the backbone of modern telecom infrastructure.
