Copper Based Systems

Traditional telecom infrastructure that started it all.

Description

Copper based systems transmit data using electrical signals through twisted pair or coaxial cables. They’ve long been used in telephone and early internet networks.

Function

These systems are best for short-distance voice and data transmission and serve as the backbone of legacy telecom systems.
